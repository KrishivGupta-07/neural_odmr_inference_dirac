COMPRESSION SCORE EXPLANATION
======================================================================

  Why V2 >> V3 (Student+INT8):
    Quantizing the tiny student adds metadata overhead (+3 KB) and
    doesn't reduce latency further because the linear layers are already
    too small to benefit from INT8 MatMul. Architecture reduction
    dominates quantization at this scale.

  Why V4 (Pruning) scores only 4.5:
    Unstructured magnitude pruning creates zero weights but they remain
    stored in the ONNX graph. No size reduction, modest latency gain
    from ORT skipping near-zero multiplications. Structured pruning
    or sparse tensor formats would be required to realize size savings.

  Why V1 (PTQ INT8) scores only 4.4:
    Dynamic quantization only affects MatMul/Gemm ops (the linear head).
    Conv weights — 95% of parameters — remain FP32 in ONNX dynamic quant.
    Score comes from ORT graph optimization, not quantization itself.
